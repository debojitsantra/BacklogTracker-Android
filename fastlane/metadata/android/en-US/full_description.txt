A beautifully crafted Material Design 3 tracker for managing anything that piles up: study backlogs, work queues, games, habits, routines, or fully custom pending lists. It works offline, stores data locally, and helps you track manual progress or scheduled backlog growth.

Features:
- Track study, work, games, habits, and custom lists
- One-time task mode and count-based backlog mode
- Optional per-day and repeat-day auto-growth schedules
- Light and dark themes
- Local offline data storage
- Import/export JSON backups and shareable templates
